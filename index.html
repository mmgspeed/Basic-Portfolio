About Me


Driven by her values and passion, Melanie Goodspeed is committed to delivering an overall outstanding experience. She enjoys keeping her focus on leading companies to the next stage of growth and innovation. Melanie is compassionate in her desire to help businesses succeed and strengthen profitability. Early in her career she built a solid foundation of business knowledge working at a Public Accounting Firm. She is Certified in Computerized Accounting, registered with the IRS as a Certified Tax Preparer and is an Active Notary Public in the State of New Jersey. Melanie has strong analytical and creative problem solving skills which she utilizes while playing Chess in her spare time. She is particularly fond of playing in New York City at Washington Square Park. Aside from her career accomplishments, motherhood has provided her with the greatest pleasure. Melanie's oldest daughter is a recent graduate from the business program at Rutgers University and her yougest daughter boards at the Peddie School where she is preparing for her college adventure soon to come.
